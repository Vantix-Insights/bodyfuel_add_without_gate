Why We Built This

Most fitness calculators online fall into one of two categories: either they're buried inside app-store paywalls, or they're bare-bones tools slapped together in 2009 with no visual design and no explanation of the science behind the numbers.

We wanted something different. A tool that gives you the same output a registered dietitian would calculate in their first session with you — BMI, basal metabolic rate, total daily energy expenditure, macro split, ideal weight range, and water intake — and then explains what each number means and what to do with it.

The Science Behind the Numbers

BodyFuel Calculator uses three validated formulas depending on the information you provide:

Formula Used When Accuracy
Mifflin-St Jeor (1990) Default for all users without body fat data Predicts BMR within 10% for ~82% of people. Recommended by the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics.
Katch-McArdle (1975) When body fat % is provided Most accurate for lean athletes. Uses lean body mass rather than total weight, eliminating fat-mass distortion.
WHO BMI Standard All users World Health Organization classification for adults 20+. Internationally standardised.

For macro ratios, we follow guidelines from the American College of Sports Medicine (ACSM) and the International Society of Sports Nutrition (ISSN), adjusted per goal (fat loss, maintenance, lean bulk). These are the same frameworks used by certified sports dietitians.

Water intake targets are based on the European Food Safety Authority (EFSA) recommendation of approximately 35 ml per kg of body weight per day, with an upward adjustment for high activity levels.

How We Stay Free

Running a website costs money — hosting, domain, and development time. BodyFuel Calculator is kept free through two non-intrusive revenue sources:

  • Display advertising — small ads served by Google AdSense, shown in non-disruptive positions. You can block these with an ad blocker and the calculator still works perfectly.
  • Affiliate links — when we recommend a nutrition app or fitness product, those links may earn us a small commission if you choose to sign up. We only recommend products we believe are genuinely useful, and our commission never influences the calculator's results or the scientific content on this site.

We will never charge for the core calculator, never put it behind a login wall, and never sell your data (there is no data to sell — see our Privacy Policy).

⚠️ Medical Disclaimer

BodyFuel Calculator is designed for general informational and educational purposes only. The results provided are estimates based on statistical equations — they are not a substitute for advice from a qualified healthcare professional, registered dietitian, or certified personal trainer.

Individual metabolisms vary significantly. Factors such as genetics, hormone levels, medications, underlying medical conditions, gut microbiome composition, and sleep quality can all influence your actual calorie needs in ways that no calculator can account for.

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making significant changes to your diet, especially if you have a pre-existing medical condition, are pregnant or breastfeeding, or are under 18 years of age.
